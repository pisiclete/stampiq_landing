Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

This privacy policy explains how StampIQ ("we", "us", or "our") collects and uses information when you visit our website and sign up for our waitlist. This policy applies only to our pre-launch website - a separate privacy policy will be provided when our app launches.

1. Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address - provided by you when signing up
  • Country - automatically detected from your IP address
  • IP address - collected when you submit the form
  • Timestamp - when you signed up

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Add you to our waitlist
  • Send you updates about StampIQ's launch
  • Notify you about early access opportunities
  • Understand where our potential users are located

We will not send you marketing emails unrelated to StampIQ, and we will not share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets - to securely store waitlist data
  • ip-api.com - to determine your country from your IP address (your IP is sent to this service once during signup)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google's infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction - request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - request removal from our waitlist at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at contact@stampiq.io.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your waitlist information until our app launches and you either create an account or request deletion. If you do not create an account within 6 months after launch, we will delete your waitlist data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify waitlist subscribers via email.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: contact@stampiq.io
